## Why Software Bugs Matter for Small Business Cyber Security

Here's the thing: bugs and glitches in everyday software create opportunities for cybercriminals. When business owners notice something unusual — like buttons disappearing or features not working properly — they often ignore it or assume it's just a temporary issue. But sometimes, what looks like a harmless bug could actually be:

- A sign that your software isn't updating properly, leaving security gaps open
- An indication that your Microsoft account has been compromised

## How to Protect Your Sunshine Coast Business

You don't need to become a tech expert, but you do need to take a few basic steps to protect your livelihood:

**Keep your software updated**
- Enable automatic updates for Windows, Microsoft 365, and all business apps
- Don't ignore update notifications, even when you're busy

**Watch for unusual behaviour**
- Missing buttons or features in software you use daily
- Emails bouncing back unexpectedly
- Customers saying they didn't receive invoices you definitely sent

**Consider a professional check**
- An IT security audit doesn't have to be expensive or complicated
