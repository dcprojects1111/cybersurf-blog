## Simple Steps to Protect Your Business

The good news? You don't need to become a tech expert or hire an expensive IT team to improve your small business cyber security. Here are some practical steps you can take today:

**Immediate actions:**
- Change passwords on all your business accounts, especially if you've been reusing the same password
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible (yes, even if it feels like a hassle)
- Check your bank and payment accounts for any suspicious activity

**Longer-term protection:**
- Use a password manager to create and store unique passwords for each account
- Regularly update the apps and software you use for your business
- Be suspicious of unexpected emails or texts asking you to click links or provide information
